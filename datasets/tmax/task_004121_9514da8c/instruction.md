   - `"median_sigma1_ci_lower"`: (float) The 2.5th percentile of the bootstrap medians, rounded to exactly 4 decimal places.
   - `"median_sigma1_ci_upper"`: (float) The 97.5th percentile of the bootstrap medians, rounded to exactly 4 decimal places.
