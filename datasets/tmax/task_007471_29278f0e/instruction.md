   - Followed by a line: `Author: <name>`
   - Followed by a line: `Changes:`
   - Followed by one or more lines of change descriptions (the multi-line payload).

   `{"version": <number as integer>, "author": "<name>", "changes": "<all change lines joined by newlines>"}`
   *(Note: Do not include the 'Changes:' header line in the `changes` string, just the actual lines describing the changes. Strip trailing newlines from the final `changes` string.)*
6. Finally, create a hard link to `/home/user/parsed_logs.json` at `/home/user/logs_backup.json`.

Ensure your Go code is complete and correctly processes the encoding and format conversions. Run your Go program to produce the final JSON file.
