I'm runing the carnival for five strait days — July 12th thru 16th, 2025. Each day we run the Tornado Twister, Ring Toss Challenge, and Sweet Treats Stand, all staffed for afternoon shift. Ride operators need thrill_ride_qualified certification, and since its a really intense ride (thrill level 9) they also need first_aid and at least 3 years of experience. Food handlers need food_safety. Ride safety rating has to be 3+ and food vendor health rating 4+. Each day's three staff total hourly cost cant go over $44, and nobody works more than one day — fresh crew each day. The ride has to been inspected in the last 6 months, no exceptions. Also the food vendor and ride have to be in the same zone on the carnival grounds so people dont walk far. Oh and game attendants need at least 1 year of experience too, I had a bad experiance last year with someone who didn't know what they were doing.
