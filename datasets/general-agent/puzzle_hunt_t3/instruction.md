Team D is taking on four puzzles at today's hunt. Their guesses are sapphire for the easy puzzle, horizon for the first medium one, quasar for the second medium one, and eclipse for the hard puzzle. They only want puzzles worth at least 15 points each, located at stations that can accommodate at least 4 teams. And they want each puzzle to be at a different station. The puzzles are also connected in a chain, so they need to solve them in the right order. Can you find the right puzzles and submit all four answers for them?
