In the strategy game Museum Heist, I need to plan a raid on the National Gallery in Washington DC to retrieve the Diamond Crown. The game has crew requirements based on what security features the museum and artifact have — you need the right specialist for each security feature, plus equipment that can bypass the alarm type. High-security museums (level 5+) need at least 3 crew members. My budget is $650 total. Can you set the target, buy the right gear, hire the right people, and submit the plan?
