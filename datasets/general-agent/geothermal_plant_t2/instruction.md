We had some issues overnight and several wells went down or into idle mode. Can you bring all the non-active wells back online? But don't touch the ones that are currently under maintenance — those need to stay as they are. After restarting a well, if its connected turbine isn't running either, bring that back online too. Here's the important part: each zone has its own safety limits. Look up the zone configurations to find the max flow rate and emergency temperature threshold for each zone. Any well you restart that has a flow rate above its zone's max needs to be dialed back down to exactly that max. And for any well you just restarted, if its temperature exceeds its zone's emergency threshold, schedule emergency maintenance right away — those freshly restarted hot wells are a safety risk.
