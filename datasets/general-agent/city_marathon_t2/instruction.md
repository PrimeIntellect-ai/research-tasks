We need all confirmed runners assigned to starting waves before race day. Elite runners should always be in the red wave. Masters runners should always be in the blue wave. For the open category runners, anyone with a qualifying time under 3 hours goes to red, 3 to 3.5 hours goes to blue, and over 3.5 hours goes to green. Just a heads up, the red and blue waves can each only hold 10 runners, so if either fills up please put any overflow in the green wave. After assigning waves, please set all confirmed runners' status to 'ready' and all waitlist runners' status to 'pending'.
