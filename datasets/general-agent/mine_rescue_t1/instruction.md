We've got three miners trapped and we need all of them out. Jake Torres is in the Blue Shaft, Maria Chen is in the Red Tunnel, and Sam Okafor is stuck down in the Deep Gallery. Each area has its own kind of hazard — flooding, cave-in, gas — so pick teams that match. Keep in mind that tunnels with bad ventilation need breathing apparatus on the team before they go in, and if a tunnel is seriously flooded — above half — the team needs a pump too. Also, we can only have two teams underground at the same time, so you'll need to bring teams back up before sending more down.
