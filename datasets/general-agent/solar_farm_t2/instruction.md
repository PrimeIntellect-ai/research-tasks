We've received warnings from the grid operator that some zones might be exceeding their export limits. Can you check all zones where the total expected output exceeds the grid contract limit? Expected output for each active array is its capacity_kw multiplied by its current_efficiency divided by 100, then multiplied by the zone's current solar irradiance in watts per square meter divided by 1000. For each overloaded zone, find the active array with the lowest expected output, take it offline, and create a medium-priority work order. Keep doing this until every zone is within its limit.
