We've got a lot of projects to assign this year - around sixty across eight schools. Can you make sure every project gets a qualified judge? Same basic idea as before: for schools that sent five or more entries, no judge should get more than one project from that school. For schools with three or four projects, the limit is two per judge. And for the tiny schools with one or two projects, three per judge is fine. Every assignment needs to match the project category, and keep an eye on capacity since some judges are already pretty busy.
