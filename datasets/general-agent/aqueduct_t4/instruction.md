This is a full-scale water crisis. The Forum, Market, Port, and Temple districts are all critically low — each one needs at least 30,000 liters. The Hill, Garden, Baths, and Senate districts are near thier 33,500 liter safety limit — absolutely no water to those. Several channels are badly damaged: Aqua Claudia to the Forum is barely functional, the Anio Vetus to the Market is in poor shape, and Aqua Marcia to the Port is also damaged. We've only got 500 denarii for repairs — so you can only afford one repair. There are also special rules: if the Temple reservoir goes above 35,000 liters, then the Port reservoir must also be above 35,000; and if the Market reservoir goes above 40,000 liters, then the Forum reservoir must be above 30,000. Watch out for misleading channel names — Aqua Portuensis goes to Garden not Port, Aqua Templi goes to Hill not Temple, Aqua Forumana goes to Baths not Forum, and Aqua Senata goes to Senate not Temple. Figure out which single repair to make and which gates to open to save all four districts.
