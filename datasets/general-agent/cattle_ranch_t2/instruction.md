Check their health and vaccination status — any overdue vaccines need a booster with today's date and next due a year out. Daisy had a respiratory infection so she also needs an IBR vaccine per our vet policy. Buttercup needs Leptospirosis. Rosie needs both BVD and Clostridial. Clover needs Leptospirosis and BRD. Daisy and Clover are both still marked as not healthy but they've recovered, so update their status.

Each cow needs a feed schedule assigned that meets nutrition targets for their breed and age — check the targets, then find a schedule with enough protein that doesn't exceed the daily cost limit. The schedule's weight range must also fit the cow. Here's the tricky part — my total daily feed budget for all five is $34. That means the combined daily feed cost across all five cows can't exceed that. So you might need to choose a more affordable option for some cows even if a pricier schedule technically meets their individual nutrition target. Once every cow is healthy, fully vaccinated, and on a compliant feed schedule within budget, move them all to the South Pasture.
