I'd love to create a new cocktail for our summer menu called "Sunset Spritz" — it should be a refreshing spritz-style drink built in a highball glass, using an Italian bitter liqueur, a sparkling wine, and a splash of something fizzy. The flavor balance needs sweetness between 2.5 and 4.5, sourness between 1.0 and 3.0, and bitterness between 0.5 and 2.0. The ABV should be between 9.0% and 10.5%, and the ingredient cost should stay under $5.00. Since this is for summer, all ingredients must be available in summer. A couple of our house rules: if you use any ingredient over 35% ABV, it needs to be the largest pour in the recipe. Also, if the total cost comes in under $3.50, keep it to three ingredients or fewer — we keep budget drinks simple. For the tasting panel, we need at least three tasters this time, and no individual overall score should dip below 6.0.
