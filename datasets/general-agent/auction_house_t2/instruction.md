Bob Martinez wants to win one jewelry item and one silverware item at the auction. His total budget for both items combined is $2200 — he cannot spend more than that. Search for items in both categories, find Bob's bidder profile by name, and figure out which combination of items he can afford (bidding at the reserve price for each). He'll need clearance added for silverware and he may need to be verified before settlements go through. Place the bids and settle both auctions. One more thing — if Bob wins any item with a reserve price over $1000, then he must also win at least one item that's in "mint" or "excellent" condition, otherwise the sale won't be considered valid.
