Ok so my aquaponics farm is gettin outta hand, theres like 30 tanks now and I cant keep track. Heres what I need — all tilapia tanks gotta be at 26C and pH 7.0, catfish at 24C and pH 7.0, goldfish at 22C and pH 7.0, and the koi in the Display Tank (TK-004) at 20C and pH 7.0. Only feed fish if thier ammonia is below 0.4 ppm — 1g pellets per fish. DONT feed tanks with high ammonia. Oh and if a tanks ammonia is at 0.4 or above you gotta reduce it first before you can feed — use the reduce_ammonia tool with water_change for catfish and bacteria_starter for koi. Once the ammonia is down you still dont feed them though, just reduce it. After you adjust the tilapia tanks, harvest any lettuce thats ready from beds connected to those tanks. Also make sure the water connections to those tilapia beds are actually turned on — toggle them active if theyre not.
