We have three orders to fulfill: ORD-001 needs a white pearl (AA+, ≥8mm), ORD-002 needs a gold pearl (AA+, ≥10mm), and ORD-003 needs two black pearls (AA+, ≥9mm each). Check the species profiles to find which species produces which color. For each order, find the right oyster species, graft one if needed, verify the bay is safe for harvesting, harvest the pearl, and fulfill the order. Be really careful — our water quality has been getting worse. Bays with pollution above 0.25 are too dangerous to harvest from, and even moderate pollution above 0.10 can downgrade pearl quality. Also, younger oysters under 30 months tend to produce lower-grade pearls. Make sure the bay temperature is between 24 and 29 degrees for the best results.
